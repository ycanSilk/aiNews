DeepSeek: Innovator in the Open Source AI Field

An outstanding representative of Chinese artificial intelligence, promoting global AI technology development through open source, efficient, and low-cost technical approaches

August 29, 2025

1. What is DeepSeek

DeepSeek is a Chinese artificial intelligence company founded in 2023, focusing on developing advanced large language models (LLM) and related technologies. Its founder is Liang Wenfeng (also the founder of China's leading quantitative private equity company, Huanfang Quant), and the company is backed by strong financial and technical resources, committed to promoting global artificial intelligence technology development through open source, efficient, and low-cost AI solutions.

DeepSeek's core products include DeepSeek-V3 (general chat assistant) and DeepSeek-R1 (model focused on logical reasoning and complex problem solving). These models can compete with world-class models like OpenAI's GPT-4o and Claude3.5Sonnet in performance, while having higher computational efficiency and lower costs.

DeepSeek adheres to the open source philosophy, with its model code and weights publicly available, allowing developers to freely use, modify, and deploy them. This openness has quickly gained widespread attention and application in the global AI community.

2. DeepSeek's Development History

DeepSeek's development speed is astonishing. Since its establishment in 2023, there has been a major technical iteration almost every six months:

  • July 2023 - Company established, focusing on large language model research and development.
  • January 2024 - Released first-generation large model DeepSeekLLM, using traditional Transformer architecture, initially demonstrating its technical potential.
  • May 2024 - Launched DeepSeek-V2, began using Mixture of Experts (MoE) architecture, significantly reducing computational costs and improving inference efficiency.
  • December 2024 - Released DeepSeek-V3, with total parameters reaching 671B, performance approaching world-class closed-source models, and officially open sourced.
  • January 2025 - Launched DeepSeek-R1, focused on logical reasoning, performing excellently in mathematics, code, and natural language reasoning tasks, comparable to OpenAI's O1 model. Its mobile application quickly topped the US App Store download charts after launch and became widely popular in over 140 countries worldwide.
  • August 21, 2025 - Latest version DeepSeek-V3.1 released, supporting hybrid inference architecture (free switching between thinking mode and non-thinking mode), context length extended to 128K, with significant improvements in programming, logical reasoning, and multimodal processing.

As of now, DeepSeek's models have been widely adopted globally, with multiple tech giants (such as Microsoft, NVIDIA, AMD, Amazon AWS) announcing partnerships to integrate DeepSeek models into their respective cloud service platforms.

3. What Can DeepSeek Do

DeepSeek has a wide range of applications, covering multiple industries and scenarios, mainly including:

Core Functions:

  • General Conversation and Content Generation - DeepSeek-V3 can serve as an intelligent chat assistant for daily Q&A, content creation, multilingual translation, etc.
  • Programming and Code Assistance - DeepSeek-Coder is specifically designed for developers, capable of automatically generating, optimizing, and debugging code, supporting multiple programming languages.
  • Complex Logical Reasoning - DeepSeek-R1 is suitable for tasks requiring multi-step reasoning such as mathematical calculations, scientific analysis, and financial modeling.
  • Multimodal Processing - DeepSeek-VL2 supports joint analysis of various data types including text, images, audio, and video, and can be used for industrial quality inspection, medical image analysis, financial risk control, etc.

Enterprise Applications:

  • Finance: Contract quality inspection, credit review, investment analysis.
  • Healthcare: Cross-modal diagnosis (e.g., combining CT/MRI images with electronic medical records for analysis).
  • Energy and Government: Energy system optimization, intelligent government affairs processing.

Compared to other AI models, DeepSeek's advantages are mainly reflected in the following aspects:

Additionally, DeepSeek's lightweight models (such as 1.5B-32B versions) can run on consumer-grade hardware (like RTX4090), lowering the barrier for small and medium-sized enterprises. At the same time, its API pricing strategy is more user-friendly, supporting off-peak discounts, further reducing user costs.

Its rapid technical iterations, wide range of application scenarios, and strong ecosystem cooperation make it one of the most influential AI models currently.

In the future, DeepSeek will continue to promote AI technology towards more efficient and intelligent development stages, empowering digital transformation across thousands of industries.
